## 1. Overview
Oncology focuses on the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of cancer. This module covers solid tumors, hematologic malignancies, and tumor immunology.

## 4. Common Endpoints
| Endpoint | Definition | Used In |
|---------|-------------|--------|

| OS (Overall Survival) | Death from any cause | All phases |
| pfs (Progression-Free Survival) | Time without disease progression | Adjuvant treatment |
| ORR (Objective Response Rate) | % Patients with tumor reduction | Early-phase trals |
| hdr (Health-Related Quality of Life) | Proxy for symptom burden | Supportive care |
| dor (Duration of Response) | Time from response to progression | Targeted therapy |

## 5. Active Research Areas
- CAR-Tcell therapies for solid tumors
- Cancer vaccines (mRNA, peptide-based)
- Precision oncology and liquid biopsy
- Radiation oncology and combined modalities

## 6. Literature Search Strategy
- MeSH Terms: "Neoplasms" OR "Carcinoma" OR "Malignancy" OR "Metastasis"
- Filters: Randomized Controlled Trial, Meta-Analysis, Systematic Review
- Databases: PubMed, Cochrane, ClinicalTrials.gov
