## 1. Overview
Nursing research focuses on patient-centered care, clinical outcomes, and healthcare system optimization. This module covers evidence-based nursing practice, patient safety, and quality improvement.

## 4. Common Endpoints
| Endpoint | Definition | Used In |
|---------|-------------|--------|

| National Database of Nursing Quality Indicators (NDNQI) | Structured measures of nursing quality | Hospital-based research |
| Pressure Injury Prevalence | % of patients with hospital-acquired pressure injuries | Acute care studies |
| Falls Per 1000 Patient Days | Rate of patient falls in hospital | Safety research |
| Medication Errors Per 1000 Doses | Dispensing and administration errors | Medication safety |
| Patient Satisfaction Scores (HCCAHOS PS, Press Ganey) | Survey-based measures | Outcome research |

## 6. Literature Search Strategy
- MeSH Terms: "Nursing" OR "Patient Safety" OR "Quality Improvement" OR "Evidence-Based Practice"
- Filters: Systematic Review, Meta-Analysis, Randomized Controlled Trial
- Databases: PubMed, CINAHL, Cochrane
