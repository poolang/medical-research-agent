# Cardiology Research Module

## Key Trials

### Heart Failure
- **DAPA-HF**: Dapagliflozin in HFrEF (NYHA II-IV, EF ≤40%). Primary: CV death or worsening HF. NNT: 21
- **EMPEROR-Reduced**: Empagliflozin in HFrEF. Primary: CV death or HHF. Also reduced eGFR decline
- **VICTORIA**: Vericiguat in worsening HFrEF. Primary: CV death or HHF. Modest benefit (NNT 24)
- **PARADIGM-HF**: Sacubitril/valsartan vs enalapril. Primary: CV death or HHF. ARR 4.7%, NNT 21
- **SOLVD**: Enalapril vs placebo. Landmark ACE inhibitor trial in HF

### Atrial Fibrillation
- **ENGAGE AF-TIMI 48**: Edoxaban vs warfarin. Primary: stroke or SE. Non-inferior, superior safety (bleeding)
- **ROCKET AF**: Rivaroxaban vs warfarin. Primary: stroke or SE. Non-inferior, reduced ICH
- **ARISTOTLE**: Apixaban vs warfarin. Primary: stroke or SE. Superior efficacy and safety
- **RE-LY**: Dabigatran vs warfarin. Two doses tested. 150mg superior, 110mg non-inferior

### Lipids / Prevention
- **FOURIER**: Evolocumab in ASCVD. Primary: MACE. 15% RRR, NNT 67 over 2.2 years
- **ODYSSEY OUTCOMES**: Alirocumab post-ACS. Primary: MACE. 15% RRR, NNT 50
- **IMPROVE-IT**: Ezetimibe + simvastatin vs simvastatin. Primary: MACE. 6.4% RRR
- **4S**: Simvastatin in CAD. Landmark statin trial

## Guidelines
- **2022 AHA/ACC/HFSA Heart Failure Guideline**: 4 stages, GDMT (ARNI, beta-blocker, MRA, SGLT2i), stage B prevention
- **2023 ACC/AHA/ACCP/HRS Atrial Fibrillation Guideline**: ABC pathway (Anticoagulation/Avoid stroke, Better symptom control, Cardiovascular risk factor management), LAAO for contraindication to OAC
- **2019 ESC/EAS Dyslipidemia Guidelines**: LDL targets (very high risk: LDL-C &lt;55 mg/dL and ≥50% reduction), PCSK9i for very high risk not at goal

## Active Research Areas
- **SGLT2 inhibitors in HFpEF**: EMPEROR-Preserved, DELIVER, TOPCAT post-hoc
- **Gene therapy in HF**: CUPID trial (AAV1.SERCA2a), ongoing trials
- **Digital health / AI-ECG**: Detection of AF, HCM, low EF from standard ECG
- **Inflammation in ASCVD**: CANTOS (canakinumab), COLCOT (colchicine post-MI)
