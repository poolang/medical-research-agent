# Cardiology Module

## Key Trials
- EMPEROR-Reduced (empagliflozin in HFrEF)
- DAPA-HF (dapagliflozin in HFrEF)
- CASTLE-AF (catheter ablation in AF + HF)
- REDUCE-IT (icosapent ethyl in ASCVD)

## Guidelines
- 2022 AHA/ACC/HFSA Heart Failure Guidelines
- 2023 ESC Guidelines for Cardiac Pacing and CRT
- 2024 ACC/AHA Atrial Fibrillation Guidelines

## Endpoints
- Primary: MACE, CV death, all-cause mortality
- Secondary: HHF, quality of life scores (KCCQ, EQ-5D)

## Active Research Areas
- SGLT2 inhibitors in HFpEF
- Gene therapy in inherited cardiomyopathies
- AI-guided ECG interpretation
