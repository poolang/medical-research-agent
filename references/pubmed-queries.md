# PubMed Query Templates

## Cardiology

### Heart Failure
```
("Heart Failure"[Mesh] OR "heart failure"[tiab]) AND ("randomized controlled trial"[pt] OR "systematic review"[pt])
```

### Atrial Fibrillation
```
("Atrial Fibrillation"[Mesh] OR "atrial fibrillation"[tiab]) AND ("anticoagulants"[Mesh] OR "DOAC"[tiab])
```

## Oncology

### Immunotherapy
```
("Neoplasms"[Mesh] AND "Immunotherapy"[Mesh]) AND ("PD-1"[tiab] OR "PD-L1"[tiab] OR "checkpoint inhibitor"[tiab])
```

### Endpoint Definitions
- **OS**: Overall Survival - time from randomization to death from any cause
- **PFS**: Progression-Free Survival - time from randomization to progression or death
- **ORR**: Objective Response Rate - proportion with complete or partial response
- **DOR**: Duration of Response - time from first response to progression
- **MACE**: Major Adverse Cardiovascular Events (CV death, MI, stroke)
- **HHF**: Hospitalization for Heart Failure
